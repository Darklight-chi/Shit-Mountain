# Shipping Information

## Shipping Process
1. After payment, your order enters our fulfillment queue
2. Items are picked and packed within 1-2 business days
3. Tracking number is updated once the carrier picks up the package

## Carriers
- UPS (default for US)
- FedEx
- DHL (international)

## Tracking
Track your package using the tracking number in your order details, or ask us for status.

## Delivery Notes
Please inspect the package upon delivery. If damaged, refuse delivery and contact us.
