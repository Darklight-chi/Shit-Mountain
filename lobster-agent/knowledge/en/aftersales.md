# After-Sales Support

## Wrong Item
If you receive a different item than ordered, send us a photo and we'll arrange a free exchange or refund.

## Damaged Item
Refuse delivery if package is visibly damaged. If found after opening, take photos and contact us.

## Missing Items
Check the packing slip. If items are missing, contact us with your order number for a reshipment.

## Quality Issues
Defective products can be returned or exchanged. Please provide photo evidence.

## Human Agent
If none of the above resolves your issue, we'll connect you with a human support agent.
